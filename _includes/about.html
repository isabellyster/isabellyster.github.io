About

  • Training

    I originally studied at Stroud Art College doing a BTEC in Illustration & 3D Design. After this I went to do a BA(hons) in Puppetry at Royal Central School of Speech and Drama. Most recently I have taken a sidestep into Fine Furniture Making. These skills have been really useful for puppet making and technical drawing.

  • My Practice

    I'm a performance maker, puppetry specialist and visual artist. I work in collaboration with theatre companies and a network of individual practitioners combining performance, puppetry and education. Often i'm commissioned as a puppet maker in response to briefs, and as a consultant at the stage of design and conception. I'm passionate about working with people through education to help realise and facilitate creative vision. Alongside this I work unravelling personal narratives, and turning this into visual and theatrical work. I believe creative work can have an incredible transformative effect, on the participant and audience.

  • Creative Collaboration

    I'm fascinated by working in an interdisciplinary way. Whether investigating scientific theories and how this can be shown through animated images, or taking a piece of poetry and bringing it life with shape and movement. I have collaborated with fashion designers, used puppetry in art therapy and toured puppet shows through libraries.

  • Future

    Currently working from my studio at Stroud Valleys Artspace, in the South West of England. I would love to create new working relationships, if you have a project you would like to talk about then please get in contact by dropping an email or call for a chat.
